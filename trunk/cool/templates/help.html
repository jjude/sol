  1. What is SOL?

    SOL is an open-source micro-blog developed inhouse at iGATE.

  2. What are groups?

    Think of it as conversation threads.

  3. How can I add a new SOL for a particular group?

    Select that particular group and add a SOL in its homepage.

  4. How to list all SOLs for a particular group?

    When you click on the link for that particular group, it will list all the SOLs of that group.

  5. Ho to list all SOLs for a particular user?

    Click on the link for the user - nickname - or the 'avatar'.

  6. What is an 'avatar'?

    An icon representing you! It can be a photo or any image. The image will be resized to 50,50.

  7. How to change my 'avatar'?

    Once you've logged in, you will find a 'Profile' link on the top right. Click on it and upload your 'avatar'.

  8. How to change my 'nickname'?

    As with the 'avatar', you can change the nickname, within 'Profile' link.

  9. How to use SOL?

    There are many ways. But we are leaving this to your imagination!
